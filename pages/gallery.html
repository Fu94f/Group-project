Earth glows at night

Even in total darkness, Earth gives off a faint green glow called airglow. Gases in our atmosphere release energy after being hit by sunlight during the day.

Volcanoes on Jupiter’s moon Io are the most extreme in the solar system

Io is the most volcanically active object we know of with hundreds of active volcanoes, some shooting lava hundreds of kilometers into space. The surface is covered in yellow, red, black, and white from sulfur and lava and it is caused by Jupiter’s massive gravity that squeezes and stretches Io, heating its interior until it basically can’t stop erupting.
